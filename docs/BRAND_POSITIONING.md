# Nexus Core Brand Positioning

## Canonical slogan

> **Nexus Core is the constitutional conduit for verified intelligence across the technology hierarchy.**

## Meaning

Nexus Core is a constitutional control architecture for governance through verification.
It connects evidence, verification, authorization, responsibility, and governed adaptation
while preserving human oversight and constitutional authority.

Current implemented repository scope is limited to:
- Python and Node verification engines;
- local/mock HTTP service endpoints (`/verify`, `/bridge/verify`);
- simulations and automated tests;
- bounded in-memory TGLR bridge demonstration artifacts.

## Technology hierarchy

The architecture may govern components across:

1. physical infrastructure, energy, machines, and sensors;
2. networks, operating systems, platforms, and data;
3. algorithms, models, knowledge, and persistent memory;
4. applications, agents, robotics, and autonomous systems;
5. organizations, institutions, governance, and responsibility; and
6. qualified human authority and oversight.

## Evidence boundary

The slogan is architectural positioning, not a claim of universal certification.

Current claims must remain limited to the implementation and verification evidence
actually recorded by the project. The following limitations apply:

- The engine is a research implementation. It is not production-certified.
- No independent third-party audit has been completed.
- The Nexus Core Constitution v0.2 source-of-truth has not yet been recovered and
  verified in the repository. Implementation does not substitute for the Constitution.
- "Verified intelligence" in this context means intelligence governed by the
  ΔA ≤ ΔV law as implemented. It does not imply general-purpose AI certification.
- Apache-2.0 license governs the code. No patent license, trademark license, or
  certification is granted by use of this software.
- Enterprise pilot evidence, external certification, and paid deployment claims
  require separately documented and independently verifiable evidence.
- This repository does not claim production TGLR federation, production Book Sentinel,
  or independent external audit completion.

## Constitutional relationship

The slogan is subordinate to the Nexus Core Constitution. It does not amend,
reinterpret, weaken, or replace any constitutional article, principle, corollary,
or amendment requirement.

## Approved usage

Use the canonical sentence exactly:

"Nexus Core is the constitutional conduit for verified intelligence across the technology hierarchy."

Do not shorten it to "Verified Intelligence," present "Tix" as a shorthand, or claim
that Nexus Core is certified across every technology or industry.
