# Nexus Core Brand Positioning

## Canonical slogan

> **Nexus Core is the constitutional conduit for verified intelligence across the technology hierarchy.**

## Meaning

Nexus Core is an implemented constitutional control architecture spanning the hierarchy of technology. It connects evidence, verification, knowledge, memory, authorization, responsibility, and governed adaptation while preserving human oversight and constitutional authority.

## Technology hierarchy

The architecture may govern components across:

1. physical infrastructure, energy, machines, and sensors;
2. networks, operating systems, platforms, and data;
3. algorithms, models, knowledge, and persistent memory;
4. applications, agents, robotics, and autonomous systems;
5. organizations, institutions, governance, and responsibility; and
6. qualified human authority and oversight.

## Evidence boundary

The slogan is architectural positioning, not a claim of universal certification. Current claims must remain limited to the implementation and verification evidence actually recorded by the project. Independent validation, production authorization, and regulated-field approval must not be implied unless separately demonstrated and documented.

## Constitutional relationship

The slogan is subordinate to the Nexus Core Constitution. It does not amend, reinterpret, weaken, or replace any constitutional article, principle, corollary, or amendment requirement.

## Approved usage

Use the canonical sentence exactly:

"Nexus Core is the constitutional conduit for verified intelligence across the technology hierarchy."

Do not shorten it to "Verified Intelligence" or claim that Nexus Core is certified across every technology or industry.
